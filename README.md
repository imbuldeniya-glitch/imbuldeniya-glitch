# Mr Arj Imbuldeniya

Consultant Orthopaedic Knee and Hip Surgeon · Founder of OrthoLongevity™

I'm a surgeon who realised the system was broken.

Most patients arrive too late — after years of fragmented care, weakening biology, and ignored warning signs. By the time they reach a surgeon, the default has already become surgery.

I built OrthoLongevity™ to change that.

It is a biology-first, surgery-last clinical framework that addresses the upstream problems most systems ignore — biology, mechanics, and capacity — before considering intervention. Up to 90% of my patients avoid surgery entirely.

This GitHub is where the digital infrastructure for that vision is being built.

Starting with the Knee Age quiz at ortholongevity.ai — a tool that estimates your joint health age across five biological pillars — and evolving into a fully AI-driven orthopaedic longevity platform: predictive, personalised, and surgeon-governed.

## What I'm building

**ortholongevity.ai** — Knee Age quiz, patient funnel, and early access platform

**The R.E.P.A.I.R.™ Framework** — a six-pillar clinical operating system integrating biology, mechanics, orthobiologics, genomics, and surgery as a last resort

**OrthoLongevity™ App** — AI-powered joint health assessment, personalised video programmes, and genomics-informed care
